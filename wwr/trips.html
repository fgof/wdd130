Wilderness Adventure

The Wilderness Adventure entails exploring on the water and off! While taking this route our escortes will guide you to our most serene spots and teach you about the wild life that thrives around here. We offer routes for beginners and to those who may have paddled before. Both have the opportunity to go cliff jumping. If youre brave enough!
*Beginner and Family friendly

Kyak Splash

Calling all Kyakers, come join us on this relaxing row down the river. This route offers a splendid journey you can peacfully enjoy. But hey, its called Kyak Splash for a reason. So keep that in mind when we meet the rapids well have to battle. Beginner and Intermediate levels are offered.
*Beginner and family friendly

Paddle Your Heart Out

Be prepared for non stop action with journey! This adventure is not for light hearted. If youd like to go the proficient route you MUST be well experienced in kyaking to ride these rapids. Adrinaline junkies will love this extreme ride.
*Thrill seekers fav

RIghteous Rapids

The classic white water rafting experience. This route is the perfect balance of enjoying the river and battling the rapids. No matter what level you are, youre gauranteed fun with this one.
*Family favorite
