Overview

Purpose

Looks like you found your way to the Skidaddle Paddle. Where we aint just country folk on water. We's educated country folk. When you come white water rafting at the Skidaddle Paddle youll learn all kinds of new things about the great southern outdoors! Amidst the extreme rafting we have dedicated a portion of our tours to teaching and watching the nature that surrounds us.

Audience

Skidaddle Paddle is here to put that taste of life back into your mouth! A tour with us is exactly what you need. Almost anyone can ride with us. You cant be too fat, too skinny, too tall, too short, too old. Well... maybe too old but you get the matter! The fact is we offer tours for all kinds of people and thier comfort levels. We offer all kinds of tours cheap tours, overnight tours, relaxing tours, picinic tours. You can even sign up here on the website. Youll see if you keep scrolling on your fancy laptop phone thingy.
